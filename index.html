About

BearClub is a musical collective from Vancouver, British Columbia. As two young individuals who began getting influenced by music from classic artists such as Jay Z and Dâ€™Angelo, as well as newer generational artists such as Chance the Rapper and Frank Ocean, the art of production and song writing has always been a hobby and passion for the both of us. Understanding the direction, emotion and impact of our music sound has become increasingly important to us as we discover new ways to fuse different genres but still make listenable music; what has always been crucial is the truth and stories behind our songs and album.

Pages, our first album release at the end of 2018, was an album we made to capture a specific moment in my late teen years; although some of the subject matter was not exactly conventional for two people growing up in a Muslim household, we decided that the authenticity of our stories would make others want to listen. As the next album gets wrapped up, we are extremely excited to see where our more mature, synth heavy and melodic songs fit in the sphere of the music industry. Faraaz and I look forward to taking the next step towards building our group/brand.
